# **User Manual — Return of the Exiled: Where’s My Sandwich?**

*A Top-Down Action RPG Adventure by Nitrogen Interactive*

---

## Description
Step into the torn lands of **_Return of the Exiled: Where’s My Sandwich_**, a fast-paced top-down RPG inspired by *Path of Exile*.  
You play as an unnamed Exile on a quest to avenge his stolen sandwich. Battle through waves of enemies across increasingly difficult levels, collect loot, and unlock new skills through an expansive skill tree.  
Each victory strengthens you for the next challenge, leading up to the ultimate boss fight that will decide the fate of your exile (and your lunch).

---

## Gameplay Overview
Fight, dash, and strategize your way through hordes of enemies. Manage your resources, upgrade your abilities, and equip powerful items to survive.  
Every level offers unique challenges and opportunities to grow stronger.

---

## Basic Controls

| **Action** | **Key** | **Description** |
|-------------|----------|-----------------|
| **Move** | `W / A / S / D` | Move your character up, left, down, or right. |
| **Dash** | `Spacebar` | Perform a quick dash. |
| **Pause / Menu** | `Esc` | Pause the game and access settings or exit options. |
| **Attack** | `Left Mouse Click` | Do Damage to Enemies |
---

##  Tips for Success
-  **Time your dash** to evade attacks or close the distance to enemies.  
-  **Experiment with skill tree paths** to customize your playstyle.  
-  **Collect loot and upgrade wisely**—each level grows tougher.
